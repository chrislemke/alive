- Distributed knowledge across AI, verifiers, humans

**Understanding is Multidimensional**:
- Proof-level (every step)
- Concept-level (why it's true)
- Statement-level (what's claimed)

- Discovery-level (how to find it)

- But we can trust the formal system
- This is a new kind of knowledge
